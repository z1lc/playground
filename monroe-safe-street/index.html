Monroe Dr Safe Streets

City of Atlanta's redesign of Monroe Dr NE, mapped

A "road diet" converting Monroe Dr from a 4-lane undivided road to a 3-lane section (one lane each way + a center turn lane / median), plus new pedestrian crossings, beacons, a roundabout, and intersection safety rebuilds. Every marker is drawn from the April 14, 2026 project Q&A. Construction begins Summer 2026 (24–30 months).

What each marker means
Corridor-wide changes
  • 4→3 lane road diet with 11-ft lanes (4 lanes kept only at Piedmont Ave / Ansley Mall)
  • Center median islands & bulb-outs added at crossings; 29 marked crosswalks (up from 26)
  • Sidewalks & ADA ramps rebuilt on both sides of Monroe Dr
  • No bike lanes on Monroe (design variance denied); bike crossings at BeltLine/10th & Park Dr SE
  • Do-Not-Block-the-Box markings; No-Right-on-Red added as warranted
  • Target speed limit 25 mph (from 30); ~30% fewer crashes expected
  • Tree removal + replacement; right-of-way acquisition at Virginia Ave, Park Dr & Montgomery Ferry
Phasing & timeline
Phase II — 8th St → Park Dr (incl. the new roundabout) + Montgomery Ferry. ~12–18 months.
Phase I — just north of Park Dr → Armour Dr. ~12 months.
Construction starts Summer 2026 · 24–30 months total.
