# customer engineer at Turbopuffer

turbopuffer is looking for someone to help our customers succeed.

**what you'll do:**

* debug customer issues, optimize queries
* write examples for our docs
* live in customer slack channels, helping to solve problems
* own customer outcomes

**must have:**

* **typist** - you can write well and can explain complex ideas simply
* **linux** - you're comfortable in terminals and reading logs
* **python or javascript/typescript** - for reproducing issues and building solutions
* **human** - you build trust and keep things productive when everything's on fire
* **systematic** - you methodically work through problems until you've found the root cause

**nice to have:**

* k8s experience
* vector search or full-text search background
* experience with the major cloud platforms

**values we live by:**

* overstep > understep
* correctness > simplicity > performance
* customer traces > first principles > hunches
* show > tell
